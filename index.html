About Us

Welcome to Karvaahh

Welcome to Karvaahh, a distinguished tour operator proudly serving Uttarakhand, Delhi, and Nepal. As accredited members of both Uttarakhand Tourism and the Nepal Tourism Board, we offer an extensive range of unique, tailor-made travel packages across these regions and neighboring countries. Our bespoke travel experiences provide travelers the freedom to choose their own destinations, while we expertly craft personalized itineraries that allow for flexibility and creativity.

This approach ensures that our guests enjoy a truly customized journey, free from the constraints of rigid schedules. What sets Karvaahh apart is our deep-rooted commitment to responsible tourism.

Specializing in Nepal and Uttarakhand tour packages set in the breathtaking Himalayas, we bring years of experience to curate the best travel experiences for our guests. We hold a deep respect for local communities and are dedicated to reducing the environmental, social, cultural, and traditional impacts of tourism. Through sustainable practices, we not only preserve the beauty of these destinations but also support local economies, ensuring that the places we visit continue to flourish for generations to come.

Since our inception, Karvaahh has gone beyond providing exceptional travel experiences. We take pride in educating our industry partners and travelers about the significance of the Himalayas and the critical role that responsible tourism plays in preserving these majestic regions. It is this blend of personalized travel, sustainability, and community awareness that truly makes Karvaahh a unique and trusted tour operator.

Chardham & Panch Kedar 16 days Group

Collectively known as Chardham and Panch Kedar Temple, Strewn across the state of Uttarakhand are numerous temples dedicated to Lord Shiva located in the Garhwal region of Himalayas.

Adi Kailash & OM Parvat 9 days Group

The revered mountains of Adi Kailash & OM Parvat are located on Indo-Nepal border in the Pithoragarh District of Uttarakhand. Both peaks bear significant religious importance for devotees of Lord Shiva.
